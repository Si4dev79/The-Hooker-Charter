Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions

Our trip prices include all fishing equipment (rods, reels, bait, tackle), fishing licenses, ice, bottled water, and expert guidance from our experienced crew. For snorkeling trips, we provide high-quality snorkeling gear. Specific trips like our sightseeing and snorkeling package include additional drinks and snacks.
No, we provide all the necessary fishing equipment for your trip, including quality rods, reels, bait, and tackle. However, if you prefer to use your own equipment, you're welcome to bring it along.
In Aruba's waters, you can catch a variety of fish depending on the season and trip type. For bottom fishing, expect snapper, grouper, and triggerfish. Deep sea fishing may yield mahi-mahi (dorado), wahoo, tuna, barracuda, and occasionally marlin or sailfish. Our captain and crew will target the species that are most active during your trip.
Yes, you can keep your catch! We practice sustainable fishing and follow all local regulations regarding size and catch limits. We're happy to clean and fillet your fish at the end of the trip, so you can take it with you to cook or have prepared at local restaurants that offer "cook your catch" services.
Your safety is our top priority. If weather conditions are unsafe for fishing or snorkeling, we'll contact you to reschedule your trip or provide a full refund. Aruba typically has excellent weather, but conditions can change, and the captain makes the final decision regarding trip safety.
We recommend bringing sunscreen, sunglasses, a hat, a light jacket or windbreaker, motion sickness medication if needed, a camera, and any personal snacks or beverages you prefer. Wear comfortable clothing and non-slip shoes. Don't forget to bring cash if you'd like to tip the crew for exceptional service.
Yes, children are welcome! Fishing and snorkeling can be great family activities. We have equipment suitable for all ages and experience levels. For young children, we recommend the shorter trips (3-4 hours). Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Please let us know when booking if children will be joining, so we can prepare appropriately.
We understand plans can change. Cancellations made at least 7 days before your scheduled trip receive a full refund. Cancellations with less than 7 days' notice are non refundable. No-shows or cancellations on the day of the trip are non-refundable. Weather cancellations initiated by us are always fully refundable or can be rescheduled at your convenience.

Have more questions? We're happy to help!
